The smart search engine for gamers


We have created an application for everyone out there, who looks to find new exciting games based on their liking. The idea is simple, you want a game, you type down games that you enjoy or genre of your liking, our simple machine learning algorithm finds the best choices for your likings. Our engine works on the base of the so called “Cookies”, so enabling them is the key to a fully-fledged experience. Overall, this simple application’s goal is to keep you out of the constant repetitive games and boredom of having nothing to play, using a simple algorithm working behind the scene.
