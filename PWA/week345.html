User Study

Interviewer: Michael Bahchevanov
Producer: Kristiyan Strahilov
Team member: Milen Ivanov


Ttv is a private TV channel, made by me, in which I broadcast my videos that I made with Sony Vegas.


Our task was to introduce ourselves as the creators of an application of our choice and to do a study on whether people like it, whether they want to add a feature or fix something. In this case, we chose a Tinder, but we had a problem because most people felt uncomfortable talking on the subject and they didn't want to interview them.
We conducted a survey and interviews (you can see them on the right) to find out how people use the app, by what age group they are and to find out what their opinion is and what can be improved. Although we were overwhelmed by many people, we were able to get feedback and recommendations for our application. Finally, we gathered all the information from the survey and the interviews and presented it in the video.
