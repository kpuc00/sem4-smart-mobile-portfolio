About me

My name is Kristiyan Strahilov (20 years old). I am a student at Fontys University Of Applied Sciences.

I like to develop applications, making websites and video processing. I have an Adobe Photoshop CS6 Certificate of Proficiency, a Computer Literacy Certificate issued by the Ministry of Education and Science in Bulgaria and other certificates of participating in Erasmus projects. I can also work with Sony Vegas for video processing.

My biggest WEB project so far was to make a new website of my school (www.sueovarna.com).

I have been practicing sailing since I was 8 years old at the Black Sea Breeze Sports Club in Varna. I have won numerous awards in different classes in this discipline. I stopped training in 2017.
